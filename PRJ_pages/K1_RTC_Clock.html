ANIMATED DIGITAL CLOCK

Animated Digital Clock for automotive dashboard


Customized Analog clock animation on a circular display, taking exact time/date from an RTC module via I2C bus and showing time in analog style.
Application is then integrated as accessory in an automotive dashboard for a one-off electric sport car (Caterham Project V).

details:

- UI design according to customer specifications, and its implementation on commercial HW
- custom graphical widget development for clock handset indication
- Initial and final animation rendering and real time indication of date time in analog style.

Phase 1: Idea & Research
The goal was to realize an electronic to be implemented on an electric one-off vehicle, representing an animated digital clock, showing exact time and date, and including also some animation for the dashboard swith on and switch off phase.
Phase 2 - Design & UX
Design is based on a customer specification drawing, illustrating colors / fonts / sizes of the various elements of the UI, to be implemented in the sw.
In this case, SW development was made on the target board since from the beginning, in order to shorten the delivery time as much as possible, and also to early begin the development of the interface with the Real Time Clock external module.
Particular attention was required to respect the precise timings for the opening and closing animations.
Phase 3 - Prototyping
This phase involves the adapting of the graphics for the final circular display, the optimization of the timings for the opening and closing animations, and some checks on the correct format of the date and time shown on display.
It was also the opportunity for a Boot time optimization, obtained overclocking the device, and disabling unused O.S. services.
Phase 4 - Final Result
Final phase foresee the integration of the prototype into the car dashboard, having particular attention to the mechanical compatibility with the dashboard frame.

Phase 5 - Caterham Project V on media
Car was reviewed on Caterham premises, by Top Gear, the famous automotive TV show that posted a video on its own youtube channel about this car.
In the video, the animated digital clock is well visible in action.
